## key features

chatmail relay servers are interoperable mail transport agents (mtas) designed for:

### privacy

no name, phone number, or personal information required. messages are encrypted end-to-end and automatically deleted after download.

### convenience

low friction instant onboarding with automatic account creation and key management.

### security

strict tls and dkim enforced with cleartext messages/subjects rejected. cryptographic interoperability with other relays.
